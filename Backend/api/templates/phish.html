Phishing Emails

Scammers use fake emails disguised as legitimate ones to steal sensitive information like passwords, account numbers, or Social Security numbers.

Smishing Texts

Smishing uses fraudulent text messages to trick you into giving away personal information. The texts often create a sense of urgency to lure you in.

Main Warning Signs

Beware of urgent requests, unexpected attachments, spelling errors, and generic greetings. Always verify the sender before clicking links or downloading files.
